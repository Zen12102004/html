Rules of Road Safety.

Go Back to Main Page

  1. Walk on right side of the road
  2. Use a pedestrian crossing to cross the road
  3. Look on both side of road before crossing the road
  4. Don't play on the road
  5. Stop Driving if you feel tired or sleepy
  6. Don't use mobile phone while driving
  7. Avoid overspeed
  8. Avoid Alcohol while driving
  9. Always wear Helmet and Seatbelt
  10. Give priority to pedestrian at Zebra Crossing
